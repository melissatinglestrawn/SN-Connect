Health & Wellness · Firm-Wide

Whole-Person Wellness, Built Into the Way We Work

May is Mental Health Awareness Month — and the launch point for a broader, year-round commitment to whole-person health at Stambaugh Ness.

At SN, wellness isn't a perk — it's part of how we deliver excellent work and sustainable careers. This month, we're spotlighting the resources, training, and benefits already in place to support the whole person, and previewing what's coming next.

The Skills & Performance Institute is launching a curated Health & Wellness learning track with new courses available throughout May:

  • Stress Management & Resilience for Professionals — practical strategies for the demands of client service.
  • Mindful Productivity: Working Without Burning Out — focus, energy, and pace as a long-term practice.
  • Mental Health First Aid for Managers — recognize the signs, open the door, and connect people to support.
  • Sleep, Recovery, and Sustained Performance — the science of doing your best work over the long haul.
  • Healthy Boundaries in Client Service — protecting your time, your team, and the work itself.

These courses sit alongside our existing benefits ecosystem — including EAP support, mental health coverage through our health plan, and flexible work arrangements designed to protect both performance and personal wellbeing.

Managers: the Mental Health First Aid for Managers session is required viewing this quarter. It's short, it's practical, and it changes the conversation.

Wellness is firm strategy. When our people are well, our work is better — and so is everything around it.
