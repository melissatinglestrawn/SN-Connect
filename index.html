This Year, Growth Is on the Calendar

The Skills & Performance Institute is launching a full training calendar for 2026 — built with every role and every practice area in mind.

Learning at Stambaugh Ness is getting a new look. Over the rest of 2026, the Skills & Performance Institute is rolling out a structured, firm-wide training calendar spanning technical skill-building, leadership development, and professional growth.

These aren't check-the-box sessions — they're experiences designed to connect to real work and real growth. Watch for monthly spotlights in SN Connect and check Docebo to explore, register, and track your progress.
